### The Complexity Trap: A Comparative Analysis of Classical and Machine Learning Trading Strategies on the NIFTY 50 Index

**Published:** June 2026 · **Status:** Working Paper

**Abstract:** I compared six trading strategies — from buy-and-hold to LSTM neural networks — on NIFTY 50 data over 5 years. Evaluated them after realistic Indian transaction costs, not just gross returns. Result: a 10-line moving average beats a deep learning model after costs. Introduced the Complexity Penalty metric to quantify this.

**Key Finding:** XGBoost gross Sharpe 0.78 → net Sharpe 0.19 after costs. SMA crossover gross Sharpe 0.52 → net Sharpe 0.48. Complexity and real-world profitability are inversely related after transaction costs.
